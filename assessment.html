Welcome to the Barbie Table


About the Page

This page was made, first and foremost, to satisfy the requirements of a homework project. But I also wanted to have fun while doing it! So this is just a small page that compares and ranks some of the childhood Barbie movies I used to love! Thanks for visiting!

Explaining the Table

The chart is made up of seven categories. The categories in order are: Plot, Barbie, Villian, Supporting, Outfits, Magic, and Music. The categories are rated 1 - 10 based on my own opinion and nostalgia.
